# Privacy Policy — Quran: Verse of the Day

Last updated: April 14, 2026

## What data we collect
The only data collected is your approximate location, and only if you choose to use the Prayer Times feature. This is used solely to fetch prayer times and is never stored on any server.

All other preferences (language, theme, colour, background image, shortcuts) are stored locally in your browser using localStorage and IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device.

## What we don't collect
- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No cookies or tracking
- No analytics

## Third party APIs
- AlQuran Cloud (alquran.cloud) — fetches Quran verses and translations
- Quran.com — fetches tafseer content
- Al Adhan (aladhan.com) — fetches prayer times
- Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — converts city names to coordinates for prayer times

None of these services receive any personally identifiable information.
